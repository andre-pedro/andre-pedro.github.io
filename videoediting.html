Video Editing

Category: Work; October 2018 - December 2020 Freelancing

As a freelancer, I did some video editing (and often capturing) for several clients, amongst them companies in the pharmacy industry such as Holon and Hollyfar.

The Process:

Pre-Production

Meeting the client (in person or via teleconference) where we discussed the project briefing, brainstorming solutions, setting goals and defining deadlines and budgeting.

Production

If video capturing is necessary (i.e. an interview at an event): Travelling to the location, use either personal or client equipment (previously determined), capture video, capture sound.

Post-Production

Using Adobe Premiere and Adobe After Effects, I edited the video footage to the client's liking and add any sort of extras that were previously determined (i.e. subtitles, motion graphics), as well as doing color correction and audio gain adjustments.

Revisions

After the first version of the product is complete, I send it to the client and adapt the product according to their feedback (based on a previously set amount of revisions in the budget).
